About us

We, Human Resources Global Gateway Private Limited, located at Hosur, an industrial city which has a strong manufacturing base of heavy industries and SMEs on the border of Tamil Nadu and Karnataka states in South India. Hosur is adjacent to the silicon city and Electronic capital of India, Bangalore city. The HR Global Gateway Pvt.Ltd having its corporate office in the strategic location with all kinds of amenities and statutory licenses is coupled with a dedicated team of professionally qualified & trained executives to meet the requirement of our clients in abroad, especially to Europe.

India is the country which has highest population in the world with 1.38 billion out of which 65% are in the age group of below 35 years old which indicates a strong work force is in vogue in the country to meet the global manpower demand in a multi pronged way. India is the world largest diaspora of population with 18 million from the country living outside for their career opportunities in versatile domains. With strong human resource development through over 1000 universities, as many as 50,000 colleges and vast number of technical institutions, India is capable of supplying quality human resources to meet the global demand. Recent days, the demand for manpower from India is on high and gain global recognition as well. This is mainly due to the talent and innovation skills to work harder have enhanced their demand worldwide.

Our commitment and how genuine we are?

HR Global Gateway Pvt.Ltd is a committed organization to meet the ‘demand-supply gap’ of manpower between Europe and India through a well defined and designed platform in a win-win module keeping in mind the social cause of Indian manpower and the ardent fulfillment of the employers in the Europe. The prime goal of our organization is to uplift the Indian skilled workforce engaging them by way of rendering service to the European firms under different categories in a right manner.
As a committed organization, we fulfill the criteria of the employers at optimum level to meet their requirement. On the other hand, employees need not shell out anything to avail the service for facilitation as we render the entire stretch of service free of cost.
